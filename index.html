Currencies (USD-CAD) (USD-GBP)

Cycles in the same sector or industry.

Above, the similarity of the cycles between the two currencies is surprisingly close as well as the determination of the Min and Max points.

We often find this same behavior of similarity between stocks in the same industry, i.e. similarities of cycles between banking stocks, or stocks in the oil industry, etc.

This gives us assurance in the reliability of the indicators when they are similar for a given industry. It also allows us to identify stocks that may present certain opportunities because they have a different cycle from the industry.
