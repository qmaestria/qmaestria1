Example of a bullish Monthly cycle with a negative Weekly cycle.

Trend:
In the long term, the indicators on the Monthly chart are about to enter a bullish cycle, implying upward pressure on rates for the next 12 months.

Currencies (USD-CAD) (USD-GBP)

Currencies (USD-CAD) (USD-GBP)

Example of cycles in the same sector or industry.

Above, the similarity of the cycles between the two currencies is surprisingly close as well as the determination of the Min and Max points.

We often find this same behavior of similarity between stocks in the same industry, i.e. similarities of cycles between banking stocks, or stocks in the oil industry, etc.

This gives us assurance in the reliability of the indicators when they are similar for a given industry. It also allows us to identify stocks that may present certain opportunities because they have a different cycle from the industry.

Bitcoin USD (BTC-USD)

Bitcoin USD (BTC-USD)

Example: The tree and the forest!

If we only use the Daily chart, we see a trend that is horizontal without any precision for the coming weeks. On the other hand, the 10-day chart gives us much more information, not only the current trend but also how much time is left in the current cycle and it gives us a better indication of the risk, namely whether we are over or undervalued.







SPDR Gold Shares (GLD)

SPDR Gold Shares (GLD)

Example of a Phase Shift.

As mentioned in our Tutorial, a cyclical movement has an amplitude, a frequency and a PHASE.

Most commercial analysis software forgets to consider the phase variations that occur regularly. If you do not take this variation into account, then your other indicators will produce false analysis results.

Great opportunities regularly arise during a Phase Shift.

Indeed, those who detect a Phase Shift in formation and who position themselves accordingly, can take advantage of the strong reactions that frequently occur during a Phase Shift.
